Business As Usual is networking initiative designed to amplify women's voices and aid in career development.

Our aim is to empower women. This panel series, Advice to My Younger Self , will amplify women's voices and offer guidance that is both compelling and valuable to those of us still trying to figure it out (*think your wiser older sister who has been there done that and wants to help).
